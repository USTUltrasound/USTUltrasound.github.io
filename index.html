Welcome to Ultrasound research group at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) led by Dr. Fan Shi! We aim to solve academic and industrial problems using ultrasound, through fundamental research of elastic wave theory to real-world applications. Current research focuses on developing new modelling techniques for elastic wave scattering, advanced ultrasonic inversion/imaging approaches, machine learning and new materials. The primary industrial application is to develop new inspection and monitoring techniques for Non-destructive evaluation of defects and engineering materials in safety-critical components used in nuclear power plants, aircrafts and high-speed railways etc. We are working closely with various industrial sectors including nuclear, oil/gas, aerospace and civil engineering in Great Bay Area (HK+Macau+Guangdong), for different research and consultancy projects. Some of the research output is directly transferred to industrial applications.
